Blackridge Group Ltd trading as Orbas

Privacy Policy for Orbas Ecosystem

Last updated: March 3, 2025

This policy is compliant with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

We are in the early stages of building the Orbas ecosystem and currently hold limited personal data. This policy explains how we manage that information today and how we will expand our controls as we introduce new services and analytics over the next six months.

1. Data Controller Information

Company: Blackridge Group Ltd (trading as Orbas)

Registration: UK Company Registration Number 16482166

Data Protection Officer: privacy@orbas.io

Legal Contact: legal@orbas.io

Support Contact: support@orbas.io

2. Introduction and Legal Basis

This policy describes how we collect, use and protect personal information when you interact with any part of the Orbas ecosystem. We follow the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. Our legal bases include consent, contract performance, compliance with legal obligations and legitimate interests.

Our services are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their personal data. If a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

3. How We Collect Personal Data

  • Contact forms, newsletter sign-ups and event registrations.
  • Account creation or service requests across Orbas AI, Orbas Freelance, Orbas Learn and Orbas Services.
  • Support conversations, payment processing and contractual communications.
  • Aggregated usage analytics that we plan to introduce within the next six months. We will provide clear notice before enabling these tools.

4. Personal Data We Collect

  • Basic contact details such as name, email address, telephone number and company.
  • Account credentials, preferences and service history if you create an Orbas account.
  • Payment information managed by our secure payment providers.
  • System logs and device details captured for security. Broader analytics data will be collected only once the tooling goes live.

5. How We Use Personal Data

  • To deliver, personalise and improve our products and services.
  • To respond to enquiries, provide customer support and manage relationships.
  • To process payments and fulfil contractual obligations.
  • To send updates or marketing messages where you have opted in.
  • To comply with legal requirements and maintain the security of our platforms.
  • To analyse trends and performance when our planned analytics tools are introduced. We will update this section before those tools go live.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our websites currently rely on essential and functional cookies to operate securely and remember preferences. We do not yet run analytics or marketing cookies. When we add analytics over the next six months we will update this notice and provide consent controls.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share personal data only with trusted service providers who help us deliver Orbas, such as hosting partners, payment processors, customer support tools and professional advisers. Each provider is bound by a data processing agreement and may only use the data according to our instructions. We do not sell personal data.

Our sites may link to third-party services. Those services are responsible for their own privacy practices and we encourage you to review their notices.

8. International Data Transfers

We store most personal data in the UK or European Economic Area. If information is transferred outside these locations, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as adequacy regulations or Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Data Security Measures

Security is built into our operations. We use encryption in transit and at rest, enforce multi-factor authentication, apply role-based access and review suppliers regularly. As the company grows we will continue to invest in independent testing and monitoring.

10. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. Account information is retained while the account remains active. Contact enquiries and sales leads are kept for up to 18 months unless we need the records for legal reasons. We will review retention schedules when new analytics tools launch.

11. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

We do not rely on automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects. If we ever introduce such processing we will notify you in advance and describe the safeguards in place.

12. Agency Contact Form

When you submit our agency project form we collect your name, organisation, contact details and project summary so we can respond. The lawful bases are consent and our legitimate interest in responding to opportunities. Submissions are stored securely in the UK, retained for no longer than 18 months and shared internally on a need-to-know basis. You may request access or deletion at any time by emailing privacy@orbas.io.

13. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You can exercise your data protection rights by contacting us at privacy@orbas.io. We will respond within one month.

Right of Access (Article 15)

Request copies of your personal data and understand how we process it.

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

Request deletion of personal data where it is no longer needed.

Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

Ask us to pause processing while a concern is investigated.

Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

Receive your data in a reusable, machine-readable format.

Right to Object (Article 21)

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or to marketing communications.
