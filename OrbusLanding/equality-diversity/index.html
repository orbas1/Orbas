Equity at Blackridge Group Ltd

Equality & Diversity Commitment

We are a small, ambitious team building inclusive habits early so they scale with every hire, partner and platform.

Our Principles

Diversity and equity are part of how we make decisions today, not a separate programme for later. Every product we build and partnership we sign is checked for fairness and accessibility.

As we grow we will continue to call out bias, design inclusive processes and share progress transparently with our community.

Inclusive People Practices

  • Structured hiring checklists, transparent salary bands and regular pay equity reviews.
  • Remote-friendly onboarding, buddy systems and access needs being met from day one.
  • Quarterly listening sessions so our team can shape policies as we scale.

Product & Platform Inclusion

  • Accessibility checks in every design review and prioritisation aligned to WCAG guidance.
  • Human oversight and bias reviews before we release new AI features to customers or learners.
  • Fair work guidelines for freelancers, educators and consultants using Orbas platforms.

Supply Chain Responsibility

  • Including diversity and ethics questions in supplier onboarding and renewals.
  • Linking requirements to our Modern Slavery Statement and ESG roadmap.
  • Documenting progress with key partners so improvements are visible and actionable.

Measuring Progress

Representation Goals

We are capturing our current team demographics and setting targets with input from employees and advisors.

Quarterly reviews will begin once six months of data is available, starting with internal dashboards before external publication.

Inclusive Experience Score

Short pulse surveys and user interviews track how supported people feel across our products and workplace.

We will surface these insights in the Impact centre as soon as trends emerge.

Accountability in Action

  • Policy Framework: Equality & diversity principles sit in our handbook, contracts and product playbooks with regular reviews as the team expands.
  • Training & Awareness: Everyone receives onboarding on inclusive behaviour, and targeted refreshers accompany major product or hiring cycles.
  • Speak-Up Culture: Confidential reporting through the Ethics Office with documented steps to prevent retaliation.
  • Community Investment: Partnering with educators and community groups to widen access to digital skills through Orbas Learn.
