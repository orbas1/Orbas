Life at Blackridge Group Ltd | Orbas

The Culture Behind the Orbas Ecosystem

A shared belief that technology can elevate human potential, guided by collaboration, curiosity and accountability.

Our Purpose

We exist to connect enterprises with intelligent automation, empowered experts and continuous learning. Every decision we make across Orbas AI, Orbas Freelance, Orbas Learn and Orbas Services ladders up to this purpose.

Our Promise

As an early-stage business we balance ambition with pragmatism. We commit to clear communication, realistic delivery plans and governance that scales with our customer base.

Our People

We bring together engineers, strategists, educators and operators united by a growth mindset. Our distributed team is small but multi-disciplinary, and we invest in the tools and rituals that help everyone contribute with confidence.

Values We Live Every Day

Customer Trust is Earned

We embed quality, security and transparent communication into every client touchpoint. Each squad shares responsibility for safeguarding the reputation of Blackridge Group Ltd and each Orbas platform.

Innovation with Integrity

We test bold ideas while honouring ethical boundaries. Responsible AI use, data stewardship and inclusive design are non-negotiables.

One Team, Four Platforms

Cross-functional squads span Orbas AI, Freelance, Learn and Services so clients experience a unified ecosystem. Collaboration and knowledge sharing are built into our rituals.

Ownership & Accountability

Everyone understands our strategic goals and takes responsibility for results. We celebrate wins, dissect failures and continuously improve.

How We Work

  • Hybrid by Design: We blend remote-first flexibility with purposeful in-person gatherings to strengthen relationships and accelerate breakthroughs.
  • Empowered Teams: Lean squads own end-to-end outcomes and tap into fractional specialists for security, legal and compliance guidance as needed.
  • Transparent Roadmaps: Quarterly business reviews and open town halls keep everyone informed about priorities, metrics and upcoming releases.
  • Continuous Learning: Every employee has access to Orbas Learn courses, mentoring programmes and dedicated innovation time.

Wellbeing & Inclusion

Our people policies are anchored in care. We recognise different life stages and offer flexible arrangements, mental health support signposting and a growing benefits stack that we review every six months.

We also track the commitments outlined in our Equality & Diversity statement and report progress in our Impact centre.

Our Leadership Principles

Lead with Clarity

We set clear goals, articulate the why and empower teams to own the how.

Champion Inclusion

We amplify diverse voices, create equitable processes and ensure every contributor thrives.

Deliver Impact

We balance long-term vision with disciplined execution, delivering tangible outcomes for clients and communities.

Shape the Future with Us

Our culture is continually evolving as we grow. We invite you to stay connected through our Careers hub, Release Notes and investor updates. Together we are building a resilient, ethical and high-performing ecosystem.
